About Us

Welcome to Shikora, where the art of tea and culinary excellence converge to create an unforgettable experience!

Our passion lies in curating the finest teas from around the globe, each sip telling a story of quality and tradition. Complementing our exceptional tea selection is a menu meticulously crafted to tantalize your taste buds with innovative flavors and textures.

From light bites to hearty meals, every dish at Shikora is a culinary masterpiece waiting to be savored. Step into our world and embark on a journey of flavor discovery.

Whether you're a tea enthusiast or a food lover, Shikora invites you to indulge in moments of pure delight, where every cup and bite is crafted with care and passion.
